Cultural Blog 2

Longterm Goal

Eventually I would like to get into video game design.

Skills I Would Like to Develop

I would like to improve on both my time management skills and my people skills.

Strengths and Limmitations

My strengths are that im motivated and I like to learn about lots of different things. My limmitations is probably that I am quite an introvert, and that can sometimes effect the way I work.

Management Commitment

I will make sure that I use my time wisely and try to not get distracted. I will also try to stick as close to the time box suggestions as possible so I manage my time between tasks properly, and dont get caught up in one thing for too long, which can happen to me quite easily. I will also make sure I can have my weekly catch up with my facilitator so I can talk about whether or not I am doing ok.

Seeking Help Commitment

Im happy that we have such open contact with both the EDA staff, aswell as the other students taking the course, so we can get help quite easily. It is also good to have the internet avaialable for when I need to get an answer quickly.

Expectations of Facilitators

I don't expect much aside from the usual just being there to help if I need it.

Other Commitments

I have quite a lot of appointments that I have to go to, but it is manageable.
